--- layout: post title: Sync Multiple Google Calendars To The iPhone published: true ---

By default, only the default calendar syncs to the iPhone and iCal. In order to enable sync for other calendars, go here: https://www.google.com/calendar/iphoneselect

Another source says you need to go to http://m.google.com/sync on the iPhone and enable the calendars there. But this doesn't seem to work for me.
