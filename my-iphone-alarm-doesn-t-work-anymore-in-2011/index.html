My iPhone Alarm Doesn't Work Anymore in 2011.

Jan 3rd, 2011

Today morning I realized that my iPhone alarm didn’t go off. Well what do you know, looks like it’s a ‘Year 2011’ bug. Apparently people have even missed their flights, thanks to the bug : http://www.nydailynews.com/tech_guide/2011/01/02/2011-01-02_iphone_alarm_not_…

Dear Apple, I need my alarm back soon. Thanks.

Update: Apparently the alarm doesn’t work only on Jan 1 and Jan 2nd of 2011. It’s Jan 3rd now, and the alarm works now!
